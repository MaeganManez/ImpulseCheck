Help & FAQ

Find answers to common questions about ImpulseCheck

Getting Started
What is ImpulseCheck?
ImpulseCheck is an AI-powered spending intervention system. Before you make a purchase, the app analyzes it and gives you a recommendation — Buy, Wait, or Avoid — based on your budget, emotional state, and spending history.
How do I create an account?
Click "Sign Up" on the login page. Fill in your full name, username, email, and a strong password. You'll receive a 6-digit OTP on your email — enter it to verify your account and you're in.
Why do I need to verify my email with OTP?
Email verification ensures your account is secure and that only you can access it. It also allows us to send you spending reports and important notifications to the right address.
Budget
How do I set my monthly budget?
From the Dashboard, click "Set Budget". Enter your budget amount, choose a budget period (monthly is default), and select the spending categories you want to track. The live preview shows your budget vs. spent in real time.
Can I change my budget mid-month?
Yes. Click "Set Budget" from the Dashboard at any time and save a new amount. The updated budget takes effect immediately and the remaining balance recalculates.
AI Recommendation
How does the AI decide BUY, WAIT, or AVOID?
The AI analyzes: the item name and price, your remaining budget, your reason for buying, your current emotion, and your recent purchase history. It then gives a recommendation with specific reasons tailored to your situation.
What if I disagree with the AI recommendation?
The decision is always yours. You can choose "Save as Buy" even if the AI says AVOID. ImpulseCheck is a guide, not a restriction. The goal is to make you think before you spend — not to block you from spending.
Why is the emotion field important?
Research shows that a large percentage of impulse purchases are emotion-driven — stress, boredom, sadness, and peer pressure are major triggers. By capturing your emotional state, the AI can factor this into the recommendation and help prevent emotionally-driven overspending.
Reports
How do I get a spending report sent to my email?
Go to the Spending Report page and click "Send Report to Email". Choose Weekly or Monthly. The report will be sent to your registered email with a full summary of your purchases, impulsive spending, and money saved.
How is "Saved by AI Intervention" calculated?
It's the total amount of purchases you chose to "Save as Avoided" — meaning the AI flagged them and you agreed not to buy. This metric directly shows the financial impact the app had on your spending behavior.
Account & Security
What are the password requirements?
Your password must be at least 8 characters long and include: 1 uppercase letter (A–Z), 1 lowercase letter (a–z), 1 number (0–9), and 1 special character (!@#$%^&*). A strength meter on the register page guides you.
I forgot my password. What do I do?
Click "Forgot password?" on the login page. Enter your registered email — you'll receive an OTP. Enter the OTP, then set your new password. The whole process takes about 2 minutes.
How do I change my display currency?
Go to Profile → Preferences → Display Currency. Choose from 20 supported currencies. Click "Save changes" and all amounts across the entire app update immediately.
